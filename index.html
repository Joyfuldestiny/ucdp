About UCDP

The United Christian Democratic Party is one of the political party in South Africa. It was founded by Lucas Manyane Mangope, leader of the Bophuthatswana Bantustan in 1997, as a successor to the Tswana National Party, and led by him for the first fifteen years of its existence. Mavis Matladi was elected as its leader on 29 January 2011. Isaac Sipho Mfundisi was elected president on Saturday, 7 January 2012. Our current president is Modiri Sehume
